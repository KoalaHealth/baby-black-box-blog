    “…every little thing is gonna be alright.”

                       – a well-known philosopher

    I am a newly minted dad who works in healthcare. From years of serving patients, nothing has been more helpful than preparation and checklists.

    We all know being a good parent is one of the most challenging events in our lives. We are tired, drawn out, have times of panic, and some (though certainly not me), weep as we try to explain over the phone to the pediatrician how our newborn has been bawling for the last 18 hours and fear they are in mortal peril.

    Try as you may, you will forget things. This is where lists, stickies, and notes (placed visibly and strategically) reach their full potential.

    To help make this easier for you, I created Baby Black Box. Our parents share summary notes, checklists, and insights as we experience them, so you can do better than all of us on your first try.

    We think BBB will be an invaluable resource on your journey to parent greatness! If not, write a comment to us on the back of a $100 bill. We are excited to hear from you and as always, good luck!
