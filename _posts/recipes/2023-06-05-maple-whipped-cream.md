### Ingredients

#### Maple Whipped Cream
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 2 tbsp maple syrup


### Directions

#### Maple Whipped Cream

1. Add both heavy whipping cream and maple syrup to a stand mixer. Whisk on high until stiff peaks form.  This will make enough to top about 8 cups of hot chocolate or coffee.
